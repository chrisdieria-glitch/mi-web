Website Creation Process

Step One

Through a dedicated chat or call, you describe how you want your website to look and work. I then translate your ideas into a structured sketch that guides every step of development.

Step Two

Once the initial draft of the website is complete, I present it to you for a full review. You have the opportunity to request any changes or refinements, and I implement them carefully to ensure the final result aligns perfectly with your vision.

Final Step

Once the website is presented and the payment is completed, I connect the site to the hosting service you have purchased. This makes your website fully live on the internet, completing the entire process from design to launch.
